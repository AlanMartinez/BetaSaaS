About YTText AI

YTText AI was built to make video transcripts accessible to everyone. Whether you're a content creator or a researcher, our goal is to remove the friction from obtaining subtitles so you can focus on what matters.

How it works

  1. Paste the YouTube link into the field on the home page.
  2. Our AI fetches the subtitles and cleans the text.
  3. Download the .txt file instantly to your device.
